Date and Time

This year, Eid al-Fitr starts on May 12, 2021 and ends on May 13, 2021. Ramadan begins on April 12 and ends on May 12. Every year, these dates are shifted back by 10 days because of the difference between the the Hijri calendar and the Gregorian calendar.

The Hijri calendar is very similar to the usual Gregorian calendar, but instead of using the sun it uses the moon. The Hijri calandar still has 12 months but they are called something different. They are: Muharram, Safar, Rabi-al-Awwal, Rabi-al-Thani, Jumada-al-Awwal, Jumada-al-Thani, Rajab, Shaban, Ramadan, Shawwal, Zul-Qaadah, and Zul-Hijjah.
