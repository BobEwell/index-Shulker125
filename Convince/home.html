Bikes are Better than Cars

Why Should I Start Riding My Bike?

Isn't it Easier to Just Use a Car?

What Can I do to Help the Movement?

Biking has countless benefits for yourself and the environment. The obvious one being that biking is really good for building muscles and losing weight. Along with physical benefits, biking greatly improves mental wellbeing especially during times of stress. Research says that biking frequently can reduce the risks of illnesses like heart disease and cancer. Environmentally speaking, choosing to bike instead of using your car helps decrease the amount of CO2 we release into the atmosphere. Another benefit that makes biking have more of an advantage than using a car is that you can save lots of time and money by not needing to buy fuel or finding a parking spot. There's no end to the list of benefits biking has on you and your community. To learn more, click here to find how biking can positively impact your life.
